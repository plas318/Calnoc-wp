    ACNL Sacramento Sierra Chapter Meeting

    3/4/2014

    When: Tuesday, March 4, 2014
    5 PM
    Where: CSU, Sacramento
    7667 Folsom Blvd.
    School of Nursing - Folsom Hall
    Sacramento  
    United States
    Presenter:
    		 Patricia McFarland
    Contact:
    		 Dena

    Phone: 916-779-6949

    Online RSVP
    • RSVP for this event
    Details

    ATTENTION NURSE LEADERS IN THE SACRAMENTO SIERRA REGION: WE ARE BACK!!!!


     Join us as we re-establish the Sacramento Sierra Chapter with our first meeting to be held in just one week!!!

     Our speakers will be Patricia McFarland, CEO, Association of California Nurse Leaders and BJ Bartleson, Vice President, California Hospital Association who will be discussing the latest updates with health care reform, BRN issues and IOM.

    CSU, Sacramento
    School of Nursing - Folsom Hall
    7667 Folsom Blvd.
    Sacramento, CA
    Tuesday, March 4, 2014
    5-7pm
    Light appetizers will be served.
    No cost.

    RSVP today so we can count on you to help get this ACNL chapter re-energized- 916-779-6949 or dena@acnl.org.

    See you there!
