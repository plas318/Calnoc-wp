    CALNOC Services

    The landscape is changing. Performance, measurement and accountability are crucial to success. We understand that nursing is the single largest expenditure for a hospital. With nursing sensitive indicators now being tied to reimbursement and data registry requirements on the horizon, there is a direct effect on increasingly sensitive bottom lines for hospitals – hospitals that must continually respond to new clinical, operational and regulatory demands. The imperative for excellence in patient care delivery has never been stronger. CALNOC can help.

    CALNOC Services Offerings

    • Training in best practices in reducing hospital acquired conditions
    • Ambulatory Measure Development 
    • Assessment of current medication administration practices
    • Support for your MERP initiatives
    • Continued Education through Online Learning Management System (LMS)
    • Consulting on how to leverage your CALNOC reports for system improvement
    • Consulting to assist you in Interpreting & improving your performance

    Why CALNOC Services?

    CALNOC is the nation’s first nurse sensitive registry. Our expertise spans over 20+ years of research and improving patient safety and quality. Our experienced team is intimately familiar with the demands of your biggest cost center and the issues facing the healthcare industry. Let us help you identify and analyze areas for opportunity and then launching and supporting a transformative improvement in your operations.
